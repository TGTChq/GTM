TGTC V3.7 — Natural Role Focus Lists

FILES IN THIS PATCH
- role_focus.py
- repair_airtable_role_focus_v37.py
- tests/test_role_focus_natural.py

WHAT IT FIXES
- Prevents robotic strings such as:
  outbound infrastructure and sequencing and revenue data and reporting
- Produces natural lists such as:
  outbound infrastructure, sequencing, and revenue reporting
- Produces natural AI lists such as:
  production AI systems, AI agents, workflow automation, and machine-learning model development

INSTALL
1. Copy the contents of this patch into:
   C:\TGTC\tgtc_pipeline
2. Choose "Replace the files in the destination".
3. In CMD run:
   cd /d C:\TGTC\tgtc_pipeline
   python -m unittest discover -s tests -v

REPAIR EXISTING AIRTABLE RECORDS
Run:
   python repair_airtable_role_focus_v37.py

The repair is conservative: it only updates existing Role Focus values that
contain two or more " and " joins and that can be regenerated from a local
enriched JSON record. It does not change Status, Relevance, contacts, emails,
or campaign routing.

IMPORTANT FOR THE ONE TEST LEAD ALREADY IN INSTANTLY
The Airtable repair does not automatically change a custom variable already
stored on an enrolled Instantly lead. For that one test lead, edit the
role_focus custom variable in Instantly manually, or remove/reimport the lead
according to your workspace workflow. Future leads will receive the corrected
Role Focus automatically.
